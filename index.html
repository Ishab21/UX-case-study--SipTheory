Sip Theory

A calm, sensory-first boba experience.

Sip Theory reimagines boba ordering as a slow, immersive ritual. Instead of clutter and urgency, the design reduces cognitive load and encourages intentional browsing.

The problem

Problem

Most boba sites are overwhelming, bright, and transactional.

Insight

The UI should match the calm, sensory product experience.

Solution

A slow, vertical storytelling layout instead of a grid.

Goal

Reduce stress and create a premium browsing experience.

Why this layout?

I chose a vertical scrolling structure to slow users down. Grids encourage scanning while storytelling encourages immersion.

“Browsing should feel like sipping slowly.”
